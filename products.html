Plastic Injection Mold

Plastic Injection Mold

APT manufactures various plastic injection molds for such diverse markets as: Electrical connector, Aerospace, Automotive, Medical, Electronic Packaging and Agricultural. These plastic injection mold types include:

  • SPI Class 101
  • High Cavitation Mold
  • Unscrewing and Collapsible Mold
  • Over-Molding
  • Insert-Molding
  • Structural foam Mold

Die Casting Mold

Die Casting Mold

At APT, we understand die casting and the post machining processes. We use DFM review to address possible problematic areas including: proper draft angle, radii, wall thickness and machine stock. We also employ magmasoft, an extremely accurate metal casting simulator, to optimize gating and shot profile, reduce porosity and air entrapment of the mold design.
